Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis (or opinion mining) is a natural language processing (NLP) technique used to determine whether data is positive, negative or neutral. Sentiment analysis is often performed on textual data to help businesses monitor brand and product sentiment in customer feedback, and understand customer needs.

A sentiment analysis can identify mentions conveying positive pieces of content showing strengths, as well as negative mentions, showing bad reviews and problems users face and write about online.

The web application uses sentiment analysis to determine the overall sentiment of the students who gave comments or suggestions about the University's services wether is it a possitive, negative, or neutral.

System Overview

This web application analyzes the student's emotions and sentiments through machine learning that will used to notify the university for the student's concern or feedback. All of the data that will be used by this application will be coming from the google form that they answered. The google form that they answered will not gathered any identification of the respondent to keep their identity safe.
